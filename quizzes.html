Question 8 : Quelle est la formule des probabilités totales ?

  • \(\mathbf{P}(A) = \sum_{i=1}^n \mathbf{P}(A \mid B_i) \cdot \mathbf{P}(B_i)\)
  • \(\mathbf{P}(A \cap B) = \mathbf{P}(A) \cdot \mathbf{P}(B)\)
  • \(\mathbf{P}(A \cup B) = \mathbf{P}(A) + \mathbf{P}(B)\)
  • \(\mathbf{P}(A^c) = 1 - \mathbf{P}(A)\)

Question 9 : Quelle est la formule de Bayes ?

  • \(\mathbf{P}(B \mid A) = \frac{\mathbf{P}(A \mid B) \cdot \mathbf{P}(B)}{\mathbf{P}(A)}\)
  • \(\mathbf{P}(A \cap B) = \mathbf{P}(A) \cdot \mathbf{P}(B)\)
  • \(\mathbf{P}(A \cup B) = \mathbf{P}(A) + \mathbf{P}(B)\)
  • \(\mathbf{P}(A^c) = 1 - \mathbf{P}(A)\)
